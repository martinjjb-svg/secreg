Securitisation Regulation - Regulation (EU) 2017/2402 of the European Parliament and of the Council

Securitisation relates to transactions that enable an entity to refinance certain types of financial assets by aggregating the assets into a pool and then tranching the pool to achieve expected loss rates applicable to the exposures by respective tranch. Typically this approach is used in relation to receivables such as residential loans, auto loans, credit cards, leases, other consumer loans and in certain cases various types of business or commercial assets.
