Transparency Reporting

The originator, sponsor or SSPE must comply with certain credit granting standards, provide appropriate due diligence and, where applicable, have made available the information required by Article 7 (Transparency requirements for originators, sponsors and SSPEs) of the EU Securitisation Regulation in accordance with the frequency and modalities provided for in Article 7 (Transparency requirements for originators, sponsors and SSPEs) of the EU Securitisation Regulation.
